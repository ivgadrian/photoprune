PhotoPrune

Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how PhotoPrune handles information when you use the PhotoPrune iOS app and this support website.

Summary

PhotoPrune is designed to process your photos on-device. We do not run ads, we do not use third-party analytics or tracking SDKs, and we do not sell personal data.

Information We Access in the App

With your permission, PhotoPrune accesses your photo library so you can review photos and choose what to keep or delete.

Photo and metadata processing is performed on your device. PhotoPrune does not upload your photo library to our servers.

Information Stored on Your Device

To provide app features, PhotoPrune stores limited app data locally on your device, such as:

  • Session statistics (for example, removed/kept counts)
  • Personal best values and streak-related app state
  • Settings and locally cached identifiers used for queue/history behavior

This data is stored locally (for example via iOS app storage/UserDefaults) and is not used for ad targeting.

Photo Deletion

If you choose to delete photos in the app, deletion is requested through Apple Photos APIs with your granted permissions.

Support Form Data

If you contact us through the support form, you may provide your name, email, app/device details, and message content. This information is used only to respond to your request.

The support form is delivered through FormSubmit, which acts as a form delivery processor.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We do not share personal data for cross-app or cross-site tracking.

Your Choices

  • You can deny or revoke photo access in iOS Settings at any time.
  • You can stop using the support form if you do not want to provide contact information.
  • You can remove app data by deleting the app from your device.
